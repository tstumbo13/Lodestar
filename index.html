Your privacy — in plain language

Here's exactly what happens to what you write:

  • Everything you enter — your situations, values, history, plans, and practice check-ins — is stored only in this browser, on this device. It is never sent to a Lodestar server (there isn't one yet).
  • Without an AI connected, nothing you type ever leaves your device.
  • If you connect an AI, the text of your situation is sent directly to the provider you chose (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google) to generate guidance, under their privacy terms. Your key is stored only in this browser.
  • Clearing your browser data (or using private/incognito mode) will erase everything. There is no backup.
  • Please don't store anyone's private health records or another person's identifying details here. Lodestar is for your own reflection.
